How does NeoX work?

NeoX is built on Ethereum-compatible smart contracts and can be integrated directly with wallets and dApps. Here's how it works:

  • ERC-20 Token: NeoX is an ERC-20 token that you can hold, trade, or use within Web3 apps.
  • MetaMask Integration: One-click button allows users to add NeoX to their wallet easily.
  • Community Driven: The token distribution and use case roadmap are shaped by its holders.
  • Use in NFT Ecosystem: NeoX will be usable across future NFT utilities and reward systems.
