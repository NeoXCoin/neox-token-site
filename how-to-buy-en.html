How to Buy NeoX

  1. Install MetaMask and connect to your browser.
  2. Switch your network to Sepolia Testnet.
  3. Claim Sepolia ETH via a faucet (e.g., sepoliafaucet.com).
  4. Connect to the NeoX website and click “Add NeoX to MetaMask”.
  5. Click the “Mint” button to get NeoX Token.
  6. Verify your balance in MetaMask after minting is complete.
